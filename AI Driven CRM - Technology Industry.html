AI Driven CRM

An intelligent CRM automation platform designed for the Technology Industry using AI Agents, Prompt Engineering, n8n Workflows, and Large Language Models. The system automates marketing, customer engagement, lead qualification, ad analysis, and social media optimization.

Project Overview

AI Driven CRM is designed to help technology companies automate repetitive CRM operations, improve customer targeting, increase marketing efficiency, and generate high-quality leads. The platform integrates AI agents with workflow automation to deliver faster and smarter business operations.

Problem Statement

Current Industry Challenge

Technology companies struggle with manual CRM processes, poor lead quality, inconsistent ad campaigns, low customer engagement, and delayed marketing decisions. Traditional CRM systems lack intelligent automation.

Solution Statement

AI Powered Automation

This project uses AI Agents powered by Prompt Engineering and workflow automation to analyze marketing content, score leads, generate personalized emails, optimize social media campaigns, and automate CRM workflows.

Technologies Used

Modern AI Stack

n8n, Mistral AI, Prompt Engineering, Google Sheets, Webhooks, Hoppscotch, REST APIs, AI Chat Models, CRM Automation, and Cloud Integrations.

Marketing Department AI Agents

The platform contains multiple AI Agents designed specifically for the Technology Industry marketing ecosystem.

Agent 1

Ad Copy Scoring Agent

Analyzes advertisement copies, scores them out of 10, identifies weaknesses, suggests improvements, and generates optimized CTAs for better lead generation and social media performance.

Agent 2

Lead Qualification Agent

Evaluates customer leads based on engagement, industry relevance, company size, and buying intent. Automatically categorizes leads into High, Medium, and Low priority.

Agent 3

Email Personalization Agent

Generates personalized marketing emails for technology clients using customer behavior, preferences, and CRM data to increase conversion rates.

Agent 4

Social Media Content Agent

Creates engaging LinkedIn, Twitter, and Instagram content for technology brands using AI-generated marketing strategies.

Agent 5

Customer Sentiment Agent

Analyzes customer feedback, reviews, and comments to determine positive, negative, or neutral sentiment and provides actionable business insights.

Live AI CRM Demo

Submit advertisement details directly from the website to the n8n webhook. The workflow processes the data using AI and stores results automatically.
